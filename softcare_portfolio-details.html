Innovastra OneCare(Provider)

The only Health-App you need OneCare is a world ultra-modern electronic health care systems with integrated electronic health records, telemedicine, practice management and revenue cycle management. OneCare offers a fully integrated suite of Health IT products empowering the transformation of healthcare systems.
OneCare provides a more holistic and longitudinal health records. OneCare Offers complete digital patient Records through connected healthcare systems. Intuitive clinical workflows that make healthcare easier. A fully integrated single shared Electronic Health Records, available in real-time, and for all levels of healthcare from primary care to tertiary care, maternity care, and specialist care.
With OneCare, healthcare providers can steer patient gratification and quality of care improvements from anywhere in the world. It can be used for self, family and friends. Patients can easily book appointments with doctors from different hospital at their convenient and pay for the services required from their providers. Typically, EHR has a patient portal that can be used by patients to access their own health records. It also offers easy communication with the clinicians.
